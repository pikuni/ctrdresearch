BRIEF PROFILE OF CTRD:

Council for Tribal and Rural Development (CTRD), a leading National level Research and Consultancy Agency has worked as Consultant for National and International level Development Agencies like NORAD, DANIDA, World Bank, Ministry of Health and Family Welfare, Population Foundation of India, ICSSR, Govt. of India, National Women Commission, Indian Institute of Public Administration, Ministry of Tribal Affairs and other National Agencies for last two decades. Council for Tribal and Rural Development (CTRD) has been ISO 9001-2008 Certified for Action Research and Consultancy Agency for Biodiversity and Social Development. CTRD has been engaged by DANIDA health project and State Medicinal Plants Board to undertake good number of consultancy assignment during 2003-2011. The State Medicinal Plants Board, Govt. of Chhattisgarh has assigned following consultancy assignment to CTRD during 2009-2011.

  • First Global Summit on Sustainable Development and Biodiversity (Gloss 2008) 7-9th February 2009, Raipur, Chhattisgarh, India

The organization has a panel of consultants who are hired on consultancy assignment basis from time to time. These consultants are nationally and internationally reputed experts on Household survey, development of data base through administration of qualitative and quantitative research tools and implementation of reports.

The Organisation has a team of trained Field Investigators who are well trained and experienced in administering qualitative and quantitative tools for Household and development of data base. The Organisation has expert Data Analysts who are well qualified to administer SPSS package for multi-variate and co-relation analysis.

These experts belong to different Universities and Research Institutes. Dr. R.N. Pati, the eminent demographer and social anthropologists with 25 years of experience on Health and Household Survey has been associated with CTRD as full time Director. The organization has vast experience and exposure for working in all the Districts of Chhattisgarh with projects sponsored by Child In Need Institute (CINI), DANIDA, VRM Foundation and State Medicinal Plants Board, Raipur. The Organisation has more than 20 years of experience of undertaking evaluation on health projects. The organization has published a large number of books on Health & Family Welfare, RCH and Adolescent Girl.
